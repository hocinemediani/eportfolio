Previous Mobility

Dubai

I had the chance to visit the city of Dubai during summer break in 2021. Despite the harsh weather conditions, it is truly as beautiful as it is in pictures. It is a real international hub where people from all over the world converge. There are also lots of start-ups in cybersecurity, which made me appreciate my stay there even more!

Turkey

I've also had the chance to visit the country of Turkey during summer break in 2022. There, I was able to visit a lot of historical monuments and cities with beautiful landscapes and a diverse environment. Going from one side of the country to the other gives you the impression that you totally changed countries, which is something I really like about Turkey!

Algeria

Algeria is the country of origin of my parents. I have had the opportunity to visit several times, discovering its rich culture, beautiful Mediterranean landscapes, and the warmth of its people. These trips have allowed me to connect with my family there, and enjoy a lot of very delicious food !
